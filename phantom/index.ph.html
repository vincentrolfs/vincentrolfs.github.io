About me

Hi, I'm Vincent! I'm a passionate out-of-the-box thinker, question-asker, and programmer. I love creating things that makes other people's life better!

I have a lot of experience building useful products with different programming languages and different teams. I'm good at coming up with practical solutions that make the users happy and keep the codebase clean.
